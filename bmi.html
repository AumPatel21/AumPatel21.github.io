BMI Calculator

A BMI calculator to get your Body Mass Index!

This was the first time I attempted to create something using HTML and CSS. Before making the calculator, I had to learn the basics of HTML and CSS so I decided to try learning them by making a simple BMI calculator. This calculator can calculate the BMI of a person and also tell them if they are underweight, normal, overweight or obese. To obtain our BMI, we just have to enter our height in feet and inches and weight in pounds as it says on the picture above. Although this calculator is very simple, it was a great learning experience for me and it helped me understand the basics of HTML and CSS. I wished at the time to make a toggle switch to change the height input from feet and inches to centimeters and weight input from pounds to kilograms but I couldn't understand how to do it at the time. I hope to implement it in the future.
